Butter Lemon Salmon Ready in 15 Minutes!

Introduction

This meal can be cooked in as little as 15 minutes, making it a convienient and easy recipe. It's perfect for when you want to quickly make dinner for your family or if you're just lazy. Whatever the case, you're guaranteed to have delicious, buttery, flakey salmon in the end. This recipe contains only 5 ingredients as well, so it's very simple to follow.

Ingredients

The ingredients below yields 4 servings. They are as follows:

  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 (6 ounce) fillets salmon
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 lemon wedges

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients.
  2. Stir garlic powder, basil, and salt together in a small bowl.
  3. Rub the mixture evenly over salmon.
  4. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add salmon and cook until browned and flaky, about 5 minutes per side.
  5. Serve salmon with lemon wedges.

Conclusion

And there you have it, a delicious, mouthwatering salmon in 15 minutes. Enjoy!
