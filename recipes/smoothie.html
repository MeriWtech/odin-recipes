Kale and Pineapple Smoothie

If you're a fan of pina colodas and green smoothies then this is just the recipe for you! It has all the health benefits of adding kale to your diet, the sweetness of pineapple, along with the creaminess of coconut milk, giving this smoothie a refreshing tropical flavor and making it the perfect healthy drink.

Ingredients

The ingredients in this recipe yields only one serving at a time. They are as follows:

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 (16 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup chopped kale

Steps

  1. Place pineapple, coconut milk, banana, and kale in a blender; blend until smooth.

Conclusion

There you have it, a refreshing cup of pineapple kale smoothie. Enjoy!
