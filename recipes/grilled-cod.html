Grilled Cod with Spinach and Tomatoes

Here's a simple and easy recipe that takes only 20 minutes to put together. It's the perfect dinner you can serve to your family and friends on a summer evening.

Ingredients

This recipe calls for 9 ingredients and are as follows:

  • 4 (4 ounce) fillet cod
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 4 pinches garlic powder, or to taste
  • 1 cup roughly chopped spinach, or to taste
  • 1 tomato, seeded and diced
  • 4 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil, or to taste
  • 4 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, or to taste
  • 4 slices mozzarella cheese, cut into cubes

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat.
  2. Place cod on a piece of aluminum foil and season with salt, black pepper, and garlic powder. Top cod with spinach, tomato, and onion; season again with salt and black pepper. Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinegar over cod and top with mozzarella cheese. Fold foil over cod, creating a packet and crimping the edges together to seal.
  3. Cook on the preheated grill until fish flakes easily with a fork 7 to 9 minutes.
